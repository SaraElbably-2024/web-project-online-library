About Our Library

Learn more about our mission, features, and team

Our Mission

Our Online Library platform is designed to make reading easier and more accessible. Users can explore, search, and borrow books anytime and from anywhere. We aim to simplify the library experience using modern web technologies.

Features

  • Browse and search a wide range of books.
  • Borrow books with a simple click.
  • Access your borrowed books from your profile.
  • Enjoy a user-friendly interface designed for all ages.

This project was developed as part of the Web Technology course. Our team worked together to build a simple, functional, and modern online library system.
