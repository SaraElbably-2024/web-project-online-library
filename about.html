About Our Library

Our Mission

The Online Library website is designed to help users search, discover, and borrow books easily from anywhere. We aim to make library resources accessible to everyone in a simple and convenient way.

Features

  • Users can sign up and login to borrow books.
  • Admins can add, edit, or delete books in the library.
  • Search for books by title, author, or category.
  • View all available books and their details.

Team

This project was created as part of the IS231 Web Technology course. Our team consists of six members, each responsible for different parts of the website to ensure a user-friendly and fully functional online library.
